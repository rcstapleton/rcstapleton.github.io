To maintain a board of directors that oversee the affairs of the corporation according to the by-laws as a 501 (c) (30).

Location can vary.


OUR Goals

To assist and/or support adult education of Hamblen County, but not limited to:

  • Students enrolled in the Hamblen County Adult Education Program
  • Adults in need of tutoring for pre-employment testing
  • Adults in need of building their reading and writing skills
  • Making referrals to the local workforce development system or American Job Center

What WE Can Do

HCLC, Inc. provides the program activities of, but not limited to:

  • Educating the general public about the Hamblen County adult education program and its partnership with the local Workforce Development system
  • Financially supporting the Hamblen County adult education program with full & partial scholarships which pay for HiSet exam testing fees of the students currently enrolled in the program
  • Coordinating the recruitment of the community volunteers to assist the Hamblen County Adult Education program in tutoring, organizing HCLC, inc. fundraisers & outreach events
  • Conducting HCLC, inc. tutor volunteer orientations and training each fiscal quarter
  • Providing academic materials for the HCLC, Inc.’s tutoring program
  • Participating in events that support the local workforce and development system

What YOU Can Do

YOU, the community are the volunteers that help us provide tutoring to the adults 18 and older in reading, writing or obtaining a High School Equivalency Diploma (HiSet)


You, the community are the volunteers that mentor the adults 18 and older to apply the necessary academics to workforce building


It just takes a commitment of your time and attention to VOLUNTEER…IT WILL MAKE AN IMPACTING DIFFERENCE IN AN ADULT’S ACADEMIC AND CAREER GOALS!
